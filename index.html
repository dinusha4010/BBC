Bees are Essential for Our Planet

Bees play a crucial role in pollination, supporting biodiversity and food production. Without bees, our ecosystem would collapse!

Types of Bees

There are over **20,000 species** of bees worldwide. Some common types include:

  • 🐝 Honey Bees
  • 🐝 Bumble Bees
  • 🐝 Carpenter Bees
  • 🐝 Mason Bees
